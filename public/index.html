About Meriton

Meriton is Australia’s largest apartment developer

For over 60 years, Meriton has grown to become Australia’s largest and most successful residential developer, having built, sold and leased more than 79,000 residential apartments and hotel suites across Sydney, Melbourne, Canberra, Brisbane and the Gold Coast. Our 4.5/5 GOLD STAR iCIRT rating, combined with a 100% completion track record, ensure our customers can enjoy unparalleled confidence when choosing Meriton.

  • Over 79,000 apartments built by Meriton cross Sydney, Melbourne, Canberra, Brisbane and the Gold Coast
  • Over 60 years of history
  • %100% construction completion on every project
  • $B$3 billion in apartment sales in 3 years
  • 4.5 GOLD STAR iCIRT rated developer
